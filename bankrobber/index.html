Bankrobber 1.0.4

Sample organizer for the Roland SP-404SX groove sampler

Bankrobber is a sample organizer for MacOS and Windows. It allows you browse your SP-404SX SD card, drag and drop samples, edit sample parameters and export your modified bank layout back to the SD card.

Bankrobber was concieved on the summer of 2011 and released as a freeware software. Over the years our website and download links went down but today we present you with an updated installer that doesn't need any workarounds. We hope you still find it useful, enjoy!

Please consider making a donation if you find this software useful. Thank you!
